Lasagna Recipe

I always dreamt of making a perfect lasagna. Even when I was little I always knew I would make it. This recipe is simpple and quick and gives you opportunity to try this perfect lasagna.

First you need to chop the onion, garlic, carrot and celery. You should cook it a bit before adding meat. After the vegetables is cooked, you can add minced meat and cook it. After meat is cooked, you can add tomato juice. After the sauce is completed, you can put the lasagna sheets and meat together.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • garlic
  • tomato
  • celery
  • carrot
  • minced meat
  • lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Cook the vegetables
  3. Put the meat in
  4. When meat is cooked add the tomato sauce
  5. Put the sauce and the sheets together
  6. Bake until crisp
